<h3 style="margin: 0 0 1em 0">Librarian for Computer Science, Information Systems, and Mathematics</h3>

<div class="row">
<div class="col-md-7" style="margin-bottom: 1em;">

<p>The Le Moyne College campus and the Falcone Library building are closed for the remainder of the 2020 Spring semester. Librarians are working remotely, but continue to offer research and reference assistance and are available to provide instruction.</p>

<h4>Contact Me</h4>

<p><strong>Appointments</strong>: <a href="https://lemoyne.libcal.com/appointments/">Schedule an appointment</a> to meet with me online using Zoom or Google Hangouts. You can also reach me by email or, during my on-call hours, through the library&#39;s <a href="https://resources.library.lemoyne.edu/askus/by-chat">chat service</a>. I check phone messages each day and will respond within one working day. My work schedule, updated each week, is posted in the calendar on this page.</p>

<p><strong>Zoom Meetings</strong>: This is the most full-featured meeting option and includes screen sharing, whiteboards, video, audio, and chat. All Le Moyne students, staff and faculty have free Zoom pro accounts, simply by logging in using their Le Moyne Google account. I&#39;ll send you the link to the Zoom meeting room prior to the meeting.</p>

<p><strong>Google Hangouts</strong>: These can be initiated through Gmail or by visiting <a href="https://hangouts.google.com/">hangouts.google.com</a>. If you are using your Le Moyne College account, you will be able to search and find me by my name (&quot;Keays&quot;) or email address (&quot;keaysht@lemoyne.edu&quot;). If I&#39;m not online, I will receive a message that I will respond to the next time I sign in.</p>
