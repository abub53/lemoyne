<ul>
  <li><strong><a href="http://resources.library.lemoyne.edu/databases">A-Z Databases</a></strong> &ndash; Browse databases by name, subject or type.</li>

<h4>Not sure what database will fit your topic best?</h4>

<ul>
  <li><strong><a href="http://resources.library.lemoyne.edu/guides/subjects">Subject Guides</a></strong> &ndash; Browse a library guide written for your subject which identifies key databases and other research starting points.</li>
  <li><strong><a href="https://0-search.proquest.com.library.lemoyne.edu/central" target="_blank">ProQuest Central</a></strong> &ndash; Search across multiple full-text databases on the ProQuest platform.</li>
  <li><strong><a href="http://0-search.ebscohost.com.library.lemoyne.edu/login.aspx?authtype=ip,uid&amp;profile=ehost&amp;defaultdb=afh" target="_blank">Academic Search Elite</a></strong> &ndash; Search this research database that covers many topics.</li>
</ul>

<h4>Have an article citation (partial or full) or just a journal title?</h4>

<ul>
  <li><strong><a href="http://wp8kk7mz7x.search.serialssolutions.com/?SS_Page=refiner&amp;SS_RefinerEditable=yes" target="_blank">Citation Linker</a></strong> &ndash; If you have a fairly complete citation, try entering the information into this form. The Citation Linker will try to find a match in the Library&#39;s collection.</li>
  <li><strong><a href="http://wp8kk7mz7x.search.serialssolutions.com/">Journal Finder</a></strong> &ndash; Search by journal title or browse by subject. Then look for a link to the contents of the online journal. If the journal is a title we have in print, head to the shelf.</li>
</ul>
