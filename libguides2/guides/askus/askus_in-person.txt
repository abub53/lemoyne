<h4>General Research Assistance</h4>

<p>The Library Services Desk is staffed with trained library student assistants who are available to assist with general research needs. In the case of more advanced needs, Librarians are available on-call to assist you.</p>

<h4>In-Depth Research Assistance</h4>

<p>For subject-specific help, contact a <a href="/contact">subject librarian</a> to help you get started with your research.</p>
