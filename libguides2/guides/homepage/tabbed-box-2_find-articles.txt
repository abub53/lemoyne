<h4>Need Help?</h4>

<p>Do you know the database you need to search?</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong><a href="https://resources.library.lemoyne.edu/databases">A-Z Databases</a></strong> &ndash; Browse databases by name, subject or type.</li>
</ul>

<p>Not sure what database will fit your topic best?</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong><a href="https://resources.library.lemoyne.edu/guides/subjects">Subject Guides</a></strong> &ndash; Browse a library guide written for your subject which identifies key databases and other research starting points.</li>
  <li><strong><a href="http://0-search.proquest.com.library.lemoyne.edu/central" target="_blank">ProQuest Central</a></strong> &ndash; Search across multiple full-text databases on the ProQuest platform.</li>
  <li><strong><a href="http://0-search.ebscohost.com.library.lemoyne.edu/login.aspx?authtype=ip,uid&amp;profile=ehost&amp;defaultdb=afh" target="_blank">Academic Search Elite</a></strong> &ndash; Search this research database that covers many topics.</li>
</ul>

<p>Have an article citation (partial or full) or just a journal title?</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong><a href="https://wp8kk7mz7x.search.serialssolutions.com/?SS_Page=refiner&amp;SS_RefinerEditable=yes">Citation Linker</a></strong> &ndash; If you have a fairly complete citation, try entering the information into this form. The Citation Linker will try to find a match in the Library&#39;s collection. This service can also find a citation from nothing more than a DOI or PMID.</li>
  <li><strong><a href="https://wp8kk7mz7x.search.serialssolutions.com/">Journal Finder</a></strong> &ndash; Search by journal title or browse by subject. Then look for a link to the contents of the online journal. If the journal is a title we have in print, head to the shelf.</li>
</ul>

<p>Still not finding what you need?</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong><a href="https://resources.library.lemoyne.edu/library-services/interlibrary-loan">Interlibrary Loan</a></strong> &ndash; Order your article from another library.</li>
  <li><strong><a href="https://resources.library.lemoyne.edu/askus">Ask Us!</a></strong> &ndash; A librarian is here to help you.</li>
</ul>
