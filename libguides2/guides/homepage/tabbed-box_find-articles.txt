  <h4>Not sure what database will fit your topic best?</h4>

  <ul>
    <li>Try browsing the <strong><a href="/guides/subjects">Subject Guides</a></strong> which identify key databases and other research starting points.</li>
  </ul>

  <h4>Have an article citation (partial or full) or just a journal title?</h4>

  <ul>
    <li>Search the <em><strong>journal title</strong></em> in the <strong><a href="http://wp8kk7mz7x.search.serialssolutions.com/">Journal Finder</a></strong>. Then look for a link to a full-text provider service that will link you to the contents of the e-journal. If the journal is a title we have in print, head to the shelf.</li>
  </ul>
