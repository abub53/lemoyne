<ul>
  <li>
    <p>Search the <a href="http://library.lemoyne.edu/search"><strong>Le Moyne Catalog</strong></a> to find books, videos, audio, and other materials in our collection.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Visit the <a href="/e-books"><strong>E-Books page</strong></a> for a listing of e-book publishers and vendors.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Search <a href="http://www.connectny.info/" target="_blank"><strong>Connect NY</strong></a> a consortium of academic libraries in New York. Books are delivered within 3 business days. You need a current Le Moyne ID card to initiate a request for a book.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Use an <a href="/library-services/interlibrary-loan"><strong>Interlibrary Loan form</strong> </a>to obtain books not found in Le Moyne's catalog or in Connect NY.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Search <a href="http://worldcat.org/" target="_blank"><strong>Worldcat</strong></a> to see what libraries beyond our region have a copy of the book. It is good way to get information about the item that you need in filling out your Interlibrary Loan request.</p>
  </li>
</ul>
