<ul>
  <li>
    <p><a href="http://library.lemoyne.edu/search"><strong>Le Moyne Catalog</strong></a> &ndash; Search to find books, videos, audio, and other materials in our collection.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p><a href="/e-books"><strong>E-Books</strong></a> &ndash; Instantly borrow electronic books through this library guide.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p><a href="/library-services/interlibrary-loan/connect-ny"><strong>Connect NY</strong></a> &ndash; Borrow books from a consortium of academic libraries in New York. Books are delivered within 3 business days. You need a current Le Moyne ID card to initiate a request for a book.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p><a href="/library-services/interlibrary-loan"><strong>Interlibrary Loan</strong></a> &ndash; Order books (and journal articles) not found in Le Moyne's catalog or in Connect NY.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p><a href="http://worldcat.org/" target="_blank"><strong>Worldcat</strong></a> &ndash; Search to see what libraries beyond our region have a copy of the book. It is good way to get complete citation information about the item that you need in filling out your Interlibrary Loan request.</p>
  </li>
</ul>
